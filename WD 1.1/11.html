The Great Gatsby by F. Scott Fitzgerald

The Great Gatsby is a novel by F. Scott Fitzgerald, first published in 1925. The story is set in the summer of 1922 in the fictional town of West Egg on Long Island. It follows the young and mysterious millionaire Jay Gatsby as he tries to win back his former love, Daisy Buchanan, who is now married to another man. The novel explores themes of decadence, idealism, resistance to change, social upheaval, and excess.
